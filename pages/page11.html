The Mamba & Mambacita Sports Foundation, honoring Kobe and Gianna, held $27 million in assets by 2022 and built five basketball courts in underserved areas. Its co-ed Skills Academy camp, launched in 2021, promotes inclusivity and skill-building. By supporting young women and at-risk athletes, the foundation ensures equal sports access, continuing Kobe’s vision of community upliftment and opportunity for future generations. As After-School All-Stars’ ambassador, Kobe championed after-school programs for 72,000 inner-city kids, 98% youth of color, blending academics and athletics. His advocacy secured funding, ensuring these initiatives thrive post-2020, offering safe spaces for socio-emotional and physical development.
